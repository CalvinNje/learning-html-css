Haile Selassie

The Emperor of Ethiopia

More Information about Mr Selasse

  • 1892 - Born Tafari Makonnen, 23 July

  • 1916 - Pronounced Crown Prince, Heir Apparent to the Throne (Alga Worrach)

  • 1911 - Married Wayzaro Menen, a great-granddaughter of Menilek II.

  • 1923 - Succeeds in the admission of Ethiopia to the League of Nations

  • 1931 - Promulgated a new constitution, which strictly limited the powers of Parliament
  • 1936 - He secured British assistance in forming an army of Ethiopian exiles in the Sudan

  • 1955 - He granted a new constitution giving him as much power as the previous one.
